This book is designed to be read alongside My Utmost for His Highest, and it fills in the gaps many of us feel when reading Oswald Chambers for the first time.

It wasn’t until partway through my second time reading My Utmost that I began to really understand what Chambers was saying. By the third time through, I realized what a gem beyond price that book is.

When I later started a Bible study using My Utmost for His Highest and added this Daily Companion, the difference was immediate. Each day’s entry follows the corresponding devotional reading and offers short, clear explanations, Scripture context, and practical application questions. The insights are thoughtful without being heavy, and they help bring Chambers’ words into sharper focus.

I honestly wish I had this book the first time I read My Utmost for His Highest. Used together, these two books make a powerful and meaningful daily practice.
