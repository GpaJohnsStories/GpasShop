My Utmost for His Highest

Oswald Chambers

Used by millions since 1927, My Utmost for His Highest is a daily devotional that is pure “meat and potatoes” — no fluff and no hype.

It is just clear, direct teaching about drawing close to God, listening to God, and serving God faithfully.

I’ve read this book three times and keep it on my desk. Oswald Chambers takes Scripture, asks hard questions, and always points us to a deeper, closer walk with God.

Each day’s reading is short, thoughtful, and meant to be lived.

I strongly suggest pairing it with "A Daily Companion" by Jed and Cecilie Macosko.
