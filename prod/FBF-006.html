On The First Christmas Day

John M. Chilson
FBF-006

“I’m always surprised by how many children do not connect the star on the top of their Christmas tree with the Star of Bethlehem. Perhaps this little story will help them make the connection.” — JMC

If you’ve got little ones who look up at the night sky and wonder what the angels sang that first Christmas, this sweet book is a joy to read aloud. It tells the Nativity story in a soft, sing‑song rhythm that kids pick up fast. By the end of the first page, most children are joining in with the verse.

It’s a small, bright, four‑color book — just the right size for small hands — and the art helps kids link the stars and angels on their own tree to the Star of Bethlehem and the angels who sang, “Glory to God in the Highest, and peace on earth.”

Each page lets you fold the print back so the picture stands alone, making it easy to show as you read.

If you want a simple way to bring the heart of Christmas into your home, this is a sweet one to share. It makes a fine stocking stuffer, and it’s the kind of book that can turn into a yearly read‑aloud tradition.
