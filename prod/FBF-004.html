Running With God

John M. Chilson
FBF-004

You know, if you’ve ever felt a small nudge from God — that quiet tap on the heart — then this book will feel close and real.

It’s made of short, easy‑to‑read stories that show how God can meet us in the small, plain parts of life. Some come from late‑night thoughts, some from books, some from moments that just stop you in your tracks.

The writer keeps faith simple, the way a child can grasp it. Each chapter points to the same truth: God is near, God is kind, and God keeps calling us to trust Him and walk — or run — with Him.

There’s light, hope, and even a bit of joy in these pages.

If you want a book that helps you slow down, breathe, and see God’s hand in your life, this is a good one to keep by your chair or share with a friend.

My signed copy sits on my desk with a few other books that mean a lot to me.
