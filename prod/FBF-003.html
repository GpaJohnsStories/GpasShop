The Action Bible

Doug Mauss (Editor), Sergio Cariello (Illustrator)
FBF-003

You know, if you’ve got a child or grandchild or a friend who you’d love to see grow in the faith, this book can help open that door.

It is unique and reads like a comic book — bright art, clear scenes, lots of life on each page — but it still tells the real stories from God’s Word.

The fun style pulls kids in, even ones who don’t like to read much. I’ve seen kids, including my own grandkids, and even adults, who won't hardly look at a “normal” Bible but they will sit down with this one and enjoy and learn from every page.

This new edition has 230 quick, clear stories in time order, so the reader can see how God’s plan moves from Genesis to Revelation.

Each story has vivid art, and “call out” notes that give more help and facts as you go. There’s even a Bible index in the back to help you find your way.

If you want a tool that makes faith feel close, real, and easy to step into, this is a good one to share.
